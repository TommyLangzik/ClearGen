ClearGen Pro Tips & Instructions

  1. SET IMAGE DIMENSIONS via the 'Width/Height' fields, 'Quick Ratio' buttons, or the 'Resolution Library' [for common standard blueprints]. Use the 'Swap Dimensions' button to flip the orientation.
  2. DOWNLOAD the transparent placeholder image by clicking "Generate PNG".
  3. CLICK on THE DESIRED PROMPT to auto-copy it to your clipboard.
    • PRO TIP: Clicking the 'Modded Extension' icons will auto-copy the corresponding alignment-aware prompt to your clipboard.
  4. CLICK on 'GOOGLE GEMINI' to open Gemini in a New Tab.
  5. UPLOAD THE IMAGE and PASTE THE PROMPT into the field as a base for crafting your final image prompt.
    • NOTE: Don't forget to add your own image upload and/or prompt details if needed!
